How to start
  • 长按右上角按钮 2s 开机/关机/点亮屏幕
    Long press (2s) on the button at top right corner to power on/ power off/ light up the screen
  • 输入 153 解锁九宫格（从左到右、从上到下,自一开始计数）
    Enter 153 to unlock Ninebox (start with 1, from left to right and top to bottom)
  • 试着到处点击
    Try to click anywhere. Have fun!
